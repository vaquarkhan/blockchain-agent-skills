# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- showing empathy and respect
- giving constructive feedback
- focusing on the user problem, evidence, and implementation details
- being open to learning and correction

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- abusive or insulting language
- publishing private information without permission
- derailing discussion with bad-faith or discriminatory behavior

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and public interactions representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and acted on fairly and promptly.
